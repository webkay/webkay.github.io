gdoc-reader

A Google Docs add-on that compares two Google Docs and shows you exactly what changed, line by line.

How it works

  1. Open the add-on sidebar inside any Google Doc.
  2. Paste the URL of another Google Doc you have access to.
  3. Click Compare. The sidebar shows a colored line-by-line diff: added text in green, removed text in red.

Data and privacy

gdoc-reader processes the text of the two documents you compare. Document text is sent over HTTPS to the diff backend, processed in memory, and discarded immediately — nothing is stored. Read the full privacy policy and terms of service for details.
